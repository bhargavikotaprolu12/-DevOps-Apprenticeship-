# -DevOps-Apprenticeship-
I was a QA Engineer who decided to become a DevOps Engineer. This repository documents my structured learning, hands-on labs, projects, and production simulations from Day 1 onward.

Instead of only studying theory, I am learning by building, breaking, fixing, documenting, and reflecting on real-world DevOps concepts.

Every day includes:

Theory notes
Hands-on labs
Docker, Linux, Git, AWS, Terraform, Kubernetes practice
Production-style troubleshooting exercises
Interview preparation
Personal reflections and lessons learned
Learning Philosophy

My goal is not to memorize commands.

My goal is to understand how production systems work, why they fail, and how to troubleshoot and automate them.

Every topic is learned through:

Understanding the concept
Building it
Breaking it intentionally
Fixing it
Documenting what I learned
